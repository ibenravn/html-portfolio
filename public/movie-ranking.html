The Best Movies According to Iben


My top 3 movies of all time

1. You've Got Mail

Combines my love of romance, books and autumn all in one movie.


2. The Lion King

This has been my favourite since I was a child - it still gets me crying my eyes out.


3. Call Me By Your Name

This movie is so beautiful and raw. Full of emotion and insecurities, coming of age and figuring yourself, and the world, out.
